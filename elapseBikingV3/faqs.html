How do I rent a bike?

First select your desired bike, then go to our Contact Us page and place a request. Make sure you mention the bike, the date and desired hours.

Should I get an eBike?

Yes here's why:

  1. They are fast! Our eBikes can reach up to 25 mph with peddle assist!
  2. They run smoothly! Enjoy your ride with a comfortable motor!
  3. You don't need to pay a hefty price to be able to ride one! Ebikes can go well over $3K!

Can I test ride a bike?

YES! Request bike testing before renting! Go to our Contact Us page!
